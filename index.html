About The Course

This course is made for the quick learners, busy professionals, and for those positively curious about the modern Bayes revolution.


The course will be comprised of content that focuses on the inuition and application of Bayes. It'll be showcased through short videos, relevant references, and downloadable code examples, that will explain, show, and guide you through the modern statistics ecosystem.


The course will use cutting edge tools which contain the most up to date algorithms and are used by modern practioners in academia and industry.
